Like everyone else, I have good and bad habits. 
像每一個其他的人一樣，我有好的習慣和壞的習慣。

Most of my habits don't have a very serious effect on my daily life. Unfortunately, one of them does.
大多數我的習慣對我的日常生活影響不大。糟糕的是，有一個習慣卻影響很大。

procrastinator[pro͵kræstə͵netɚ]

I am a procrastinator.  I always put off doing things until the last possible moment.  
我是一個拖延者，凡事都拖到最後非做不可時才做。

I picked up this bad habit from my brother. 
我是從我哥哥那裡學來的這個壞習慣。

This habit really causes me problem, especially when I have many assignments due about the same time.  這一習慣的確是問題，尤其是當我有很多作業要同時繳交的時候。

My life seems to run in cycles of leisurely heaven and incredibly frantic hell.  I wish I could get rid of this terrible habit.
 由於凡事皆拖，所以我的生活就在悠閒的天堂與極端焦慮的地獄兩者之間循環。但願我能去掉這個很壞的習慣。/
